We have yoga classes for Everyone

Beginners

Are you new to practicing yoga? Then this class is perfect for you. It's based on Vinyasa, but it's not as intimidating or intense. This class is best for the days when you want to focus on awareness of movement and breath at a comfortable pace. The gentle transitions between poses encourage mindfulness, resilience, and self-compassion. It's also an excellent way to reset your nervous system if you've been feeling anxious or overwhelmed.

Vinyasa

For students seeking to discover movement, creativity, playfulness, and a profound connection to their breath and body, Vinyasa is a superb option. This particular style encourages imaginative and dynamic movement in sync with your breath's rhythm. Each class will have distinct sequences, and it's probable you'll encounter fresh poses and transitions frequently.

Power Yoga

Our most challenging practise! A yoga flow that is invigorating, dynamic, and empowering. This class is programmed to develop your core strength and stability as you delve into a variety of postures and test out different movements. We recommend that you have previous knowledge of yoga.
